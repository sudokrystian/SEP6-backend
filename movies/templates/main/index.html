Welcome to the Movies API

Currently available endpoints:

  • Get all movies at: http://localhost:8000/movies
  • Get a movie by id at: http://localhost:8000/movies/{movie_id}
  • Get all people at: http://localhost:8000/people
  • Get a person by id at: http://localhost:8000/people/{person_id}
  • Get all directors at: http://localhost:8000/directors
  • Get a director by the person id: http://localhost:8000/directors/people/{person_id}
  • Get a director by the movie id: http://localhost:8000/directors/movies/{movie_id}
